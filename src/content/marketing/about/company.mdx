## Vision

Apotheon.ai unifies marketing, product, and operations teams through a single composable platform.
We prioritize responsible data usage, secure collaboration, and measurable outcomes with every
release.

## Operating Principles

1. **Build for governance from day zero.** Compliance, accessibility, and observability are core
   requirements, not later-stage add-ons.
2. **Invest in automation.** Every manual workflow becomes a scriptable pipeline the moment it slows
   down our teams or customers.
3. **Design for humans.** Clear language, inclusive experiences, and fast feedback loops help us
   deliver platforms people love.
